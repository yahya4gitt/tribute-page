Mohamed Ali High Profile Fights

ALI VS. SONNY LISTON I

Date: February 25, 1964

Result: Win - Technical Knockout in 7 rounds

Location: Miami Beach, Florida

Notes: Ali won his first world heavyweight title in this fight, shocking the boxing world.

ALI VS. SONNY LISTON II

Date: May 25, 1965

Result: Win - Knockout in 1 round

Location: Lewiston, Maine

Notes: Famous for the "Phantom Punch," this fight remains one of the most controversial in boxing history.

ALI VS. JOE FRAZIER I (The Fight of the Century)

Date: March 8, 1971

Result: Loss - Unanimous Decision in 15 rounds

Location: Madison Square Garden, New York

Notes: This fight was the first of three bouts between Ali and Frazier and is considered one of the greatest in boxing history.

ALI VS. GEORGE FOREMAN (The Rumble in the Jungle)

Date: October 30, 1974

Result: Win - Knockout in 8 rounds

Location: Kinshasa, Zaire

Notes: Ali regained the world heavyweight title with a legendary performance, employing the "rope-a-dope" strategy.

ALI VS. JOE FRAZIER III (The Thrilla in Manila)

Date: October 1, 1975

Result: Win - Technical Knockout in 14 rounds

Location: Manila, Philippines

Notes: The third and final bout between Ali and Frazier, widely regarded as one of the greatest boxing matches of all time.

ALI VS. LEON SPINKS II

Date: September 15, 1978

Result: Win - Unanimous Decision in 15 rounds

Location: New Orleans, Louisiana

Notes: Ali regained the world heavyweight title for a record third time in this fight.
